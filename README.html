The Lady And The Mirror

Every step she did, made her more hopeless and drained. She succummed and fell to her knees crying out loud.

Engulfed by the feeling of darkness, she finally looked up and saw the brightest light she ever saw.

  • She ran closer and saw her own face, deeply saddend.

  • To her surpside it was her that was beaming the brighest light!
